What is Elona Toneruoy?

Elona Toneruoy was made for the Brackeys Game Jam 2022.2, our first ever submission to this jam.

Try to get out of the store. Explore the factory, the shop, and the VIP lounge. Take care of your friends, you don't want to end up alone!
