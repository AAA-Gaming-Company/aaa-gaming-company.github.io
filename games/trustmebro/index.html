What is Trust Me Bro?

Trust Me Bro was made for the Brackeys Game Jam 2025.1, this was our third entry into this wonderful competition.

Are you sick of never getting a date? Play this super realistic "dating sim" where nothing can go wrong... Will Buff McManlyface find true love?

Aim with mouse, shift to sprint! Use WASD (or equivalent) to move around, you can also use the arrow keys if that's your thing.
