Welcome to AAA Gaming Company, a team of passionate developers and artists who have one goal: creating games that you'll love to play. Sure, we might have "Company" in the name, but let's be honest - it's more of a group of "talented" individuals coming together to craft something special.

How It All StAAArted

Back in 2018, three friends, Aidan, Arlo, and Alex, found themselves sitting around with nothing but ideas, passion, and absolutely no clue how to make games. You see, the dream was clear: to create something amazing, something people would want to play. The only problem? We had no idea how to actually make that happen. We didn't know how to code, we didn't understand game design, and we didn't have a clue about the magic that makes a game great. But you know what they say, you've got to start somewhere.

So, after a fair bit of trial, error, and late-night Googling, we "made" our first game. It didn't exactly set the world on fire, but hey, it ran (kind of). That little game was proof that we had something, and we haven't stopped since.

The TeAAAm Today

Fast forward a few years and our team has grown, adding fresh faces who bring their own flair to the table. We're still the same bunch of passionate developers and artists, but now, we've got a little more know-how under our belts. While Aidan, Arlo, and Alex might've been the founders, we've all chipped in to make this dream a reality - though some of us are a little less involved these days.

What MAAAkes Us Different?

We're not a faceless corporation with a thousand employees. We're just a bunch of people who genuinely love games and are determined to create experiences that will bring joy to players. From design to development, every line of code and every pixel on screen has a piece of us in it.

Our games aren't always perfect (who's are?), but they're always built with heart. We might still make some mistakes, but that's all part of the fun, right? And believe us, we're learning and growing with every project we take on.
